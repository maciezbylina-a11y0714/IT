Our Projects

Allyson Tech Solutions is a professional fullstack development IT company specializing in
cutting-edge web applications, automation solutions, and data-driven technologies.
We deliver end-to-end development services from frontend to backend, ensuring scalable and robust solutions.

BrasilAPI - API de Dados Brasileiros

Plataforma de API RESTful desenvolvida para disponibilizar dados públicos brasileiros de forma acessível. Integração com dados do IBGE, CEP, CNPJ e outras fontes governamentais para facilitar o desenvolvimento de aplicações brasileiras.
Querido Diário - Plataforma Cívica

Sistema de web scraping e análise de dados para tornar os diários oficiais brasileiros acessíveis. Desenvolvido com Python, oferece coleta automatizada de dados governamentais e análise de transparência pública para cidadãos brasileiros.
Login Cidadão - Autenticação Governamental

Sistema de autenticação unificada para serviços públicos brasileiros. Plataforma desenvolvida para integração com sistemas governamentais, oferecendo login seguro e integração com APIs de serviços públicos do Brasil.
Serenata de Amor - IA para Controle Social

Plataforma de inteligência artificial para controle social da administração pública brasileira. Utiliza machine learning e análise de dados para identificar irregularidades em gastos públicos e promover transparência governamental.
EU Open Data Portal - European Data Platform

Official European Union open data portal providing centralized access to public sector information across EU member states. Features RESTful APIs (CKAN, SPARQL, MQA), data harvesting capabilities, and metadata quality assessments for developers and researchers.
Brasil.IO - Dados Públicos Acessíveis

Plataforma backend para coleta e disponibilização de datasets públicos brasileiros. Sistema de web scraping e processamento de dados que transforma informações governamentais em formatos acessíveis para desenvolvedores e pesquisadores brasileiros.

FAQ

+

How much will it cost?

To get a better idea of the cost of what you want to build, give us a quick phone call. We’ll ask you a few questions about the nature of the site, what sort of interactivity the site will have, your graphic design needs, etc. Then we’ll be able to give you a ballpark figure. If you’re still interested, we’ll come to your place of business and come up with a firm quote.

+

How long it takes to design?

The time limit of any assignment is normally dictated by the client. If you have any time limit in mind we will attempt to assemble it for you. The main general delay in the making of a website is waiting for the content of the pages to be sent to us by the client.

+

How do I associate with Allyson Tech Solutions?

The process begins when you contact us with your requirements. We analyze your requirements and respond to you. On the basis of the further discussion, you can choose an engagement model that suits you the best. After that, we begin the process of development.

+

Can you help my current site look more professional?

Yes. Give us your requirements and we have experienced expertise to help you give a new professional look that really wonders!

+

When do I pay?

For most projects, equal payments are made at the start, midway, and the end of the project, but we can work with you to set up a schedule that meets your needs. We understand that this is a big investment and want to help you budget for the expense in whatever way possible. We accept checks, PayPal, and all major credit cards.
